Account Deletion Request

⚠️ Important: Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently removed from our systems.

How to Request Account Deletion

Option 1: Via Email (Recommended)

Send an email to:

Email: [your-email@example.com]

Subject: "Account Deletion Request - iTestified"

Include the following information in your email:

  • Your registered email address
  • Your full name (if applicable)
  • The phrase: "I request permanent deletion of my iTestified account and all associated data"
  • Optional: Reason for deletion (helps us improve)

Option 2: Via In-App

Open the iTestified app and follow these steps:

  1. Go to Settings (gear icon)
  2. Tap on Account
  3. Select Delete Account
  4. Confirm your choice

What Happens After Your Request

  1. Verification: We'll verify your identity within 24 hours
  2. Processing: Your account will be scheduled for deletion
  3. Confirmation: You'll receive a confirmation email
  4. Completion: All data will be permanently deleted within 30 days

Data Retention Exceptions

Please note that we may retain certain information when required by law, including:

  • Transaction records for financial compliance
  • Information needed for legal disputes
  • Aggregated, anonymized data for analytics

Need Help?

If you have questions about account deletion or need assistance, contact us at [support-email@example.com]
