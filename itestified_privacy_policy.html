Privacy Policy for iTestified

Last updated: December 09, 2025

iTestified ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by iTestified.

By using our mobile application (the "App"), you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you use Google Sign-In to authenticate, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Profile picture URL (optional)
  • Google user ID

1.2 Device and Usage Information

We automatically collect certain information when you use our App:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • App usage statistics
  • Crash reports and error logs
  • IP address

1.3 Media Information

When you save inspirational pictures:

  • We request access to your photo library only to save images
  • We do not access, read, or upload your existing photos without permission
  • Saved images remain locally on your device

1.4 Advertising Information

We use Google AdMob for advertisements, which may collect:

  • Device advertising ID
  • Non-personal demographic information
  • Interactions with advertisements

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Providing and maintaining the App
  • Authenticating your Google account
  • Personalizing your experience
  • Saving inspirational pictures to your device gallery
  • Displaying relevant advertisements
  • Analyzing app usage to improve features
  • Troubleshooting technical issues
  • Complying with legal obligations

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Data Storage

  • Google authentication data is stored securely on Google servers
  • App preferences are stored locally on your device
  • Saved images are stored in your device's photo gallery
  • We do not maintain a separate database of user content

3.2 Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Google Services

We use Google Sign-In for authentication. Google's privacy policy applies to information provided to Google: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4.2 Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect information as described in their privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

4.3 Data Sharing with Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information when required by law or legal process.

5. User Rights and Choices

5.1 Access and Control

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information, and withdraw consent for data processing.

5.2 Opt-Out Options

  • You can revoke Google Sign-In permissions through your Google account settings
  • You can reset your advertising ID through device settings
  • You can disable photo library access through device settings

5.3 Account Deletion

To delete your account and associated data, please contact us at the email below.
